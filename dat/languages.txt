Common / Common is spoken by most creatures in D&D.
Dwarvish / Dwarvish is full of hard consonants and guttural sounds.
Elvish / Elvish is fluid, with subtle intonations and intricate grammar.
Giant / Giant is spoken by Ogres and Giants.
Gnomish / The Gnomish language is renowned for its technical treatises and its catalogues of knowledge.
Goblin / Goblin is spoken by Goblinoids.
Halfling / Halfing is a secretive language with a strong oral tradition.
Orc / Orc is a harsh, grating language with hard consonants.
Abyssal / Abyssal is spoken by demons.
Celestial / Celestial is spoken by celestials.
Draconic / Draconic is thought to be one of the oldest languages and is often used in the study of magic.
Deep Speech / Deep Speech is spoken by mind flayers and beholders.
Infernal / Infernal is spoken by devils.
Primordial / Primordial is spoken by elementals.
Sylvan / Sylvan is spoken by fey creatures.
Undercommon / Undercommon is spoken by traders in the underdark.
